How to reduce calories when eating fast food

🥗

Choose bowls over burritos

At Chipotle, a burrito bowl (525 kcal) has 275 fewer calories than a burrito (800 kcal) — purely from removing the tortilla. Same filling, 34% fewer calories.

🥤

Skip the sugary drinks

A large Coke adds 310 calories to any meal — almost the same as a small burger. Switching to water saves those calories without any sacrifice in food satisfaction.

Frequently asked questions

How many calories are in a Chipotle burrito?

A typical Chipotle chicken burrito contains around 800–1,000 calories depending on your toppings. The tortilla alone adds approximately 320 calories. Switching to a burrito bowl (no tortilla) saves 275 calories — a burrito bowl with chicken averages around 525 calories and provides the same amount of protein.

What is the lowest calorie item at McDonald's?

The Caesar Salad without dressing is one of the lowest calorie options at McDonald's at just 90 calories. Among sandwiches, the McDouble at 400 calories offers the best protein-to-calorie ratio. Small French Fries at 230 calories are significantly better than large fries at 490 calories.

What is the lowest calorie Subway sandwich?

The Veggie Delite 6-inch is Subway's lowest calorie sandwich at just 200 calories. Among meat options, the Turkey Breast 6-inch at 280 calories is the best choice. Switching from white to 9-grain wheat bread saves around 20 calories and adds more fiber and nutrients.

How many calories are in a KFC bucket?

A KFC Original Recipe chicken breast contains 390 calories per piece. A thigh has 280 calories. Per 100g, KFC Original Recipe chicken contains approximately 295 calories. The Extra Crispy version adds about 25% more calories due to the thicker batter coating.

Can I eat fast food and still lose weight?

Yes — weight loss depends on your total daily calorie intake, not on which specific foods you eat. You can eat fast food and still lose weight as long as you stay within your daily calorie deficit.
